    TRANSMISSION TROUBLESHOOTING

     

    You should have your transmission checked if you experience the following symptoms:

     

    Your transmission is not getting or staying in gear

     

    Shifting gears is sluggish or difficult

     

    You hear a grinding, buzzing, or clicking sound when engaging gears or while in 4-wheel drive

     

    You are having problems with the clutch

     

    Your vehicle will not move in reverse

     

    Your transmission delays when shifting

     

    The vehicle overheats

     

    Transmission fluid does not break down as fast as engine oil. If you are going through transmission fluid by the liters ? you most likely have a leak
